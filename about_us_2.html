    • WHO WE ARE?

    In the year 1989, Mr. Tarachand Mehta (Jain), a visionary entrepreneur, graced the bustling city of Chennai with his presence, brimming with the ambition to craft magnificent jewellery that would sing to the soul of every woman. And thus, was born the legacy of Khazanchi Jewellers.

    With the establishment of its first showroom on NSC Bose Road, Chennai, Mr. Mehta revolutionized the organized jewellery retail industry in India, paving the way for a new era of excellence and sophistication.

    For over three decades, Khazanchi has relentlessly pursued the pinnacle of perfection, continually pushing the boundaries of innovation and excellence. Today, the legacy of Khazanchi Jewellers continues to thrive, captivating the hearts of women everywhere with its timeless beauty and unparalleled craftsmanship.

    At the heart of Khazanchi lies a profound understanding that a woman's bond with her jewellery is more than just the mere beauty of its design or the rarity of its gems. It is an extension of her essence, a reflection of her style, and a symbol of her spirit. This unwavering commitment to sincerity has driven Khazanchi to seek out the finest artisans in the land, to bring to life a collection of hand-crafted gold and diamond jewellery, each piece an exquisite work of art, each a one-of-a-kind masterpiece.
